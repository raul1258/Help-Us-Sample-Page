Help Us

Mental illness

Mental illness isn't a one day process, it is a slow eater, keeps effecting negatively gradually resulting in a prolonged illness.

Importance

Mental Health is important, turning into a fantasy due to the hectice schedule of today's generation and lack of awareness among them.

Types of effects

Lack of awareness results in effects like depression ,bad addictions & several disorders.

Way to Solve

Discussing issues, having love from people around results in a drastic increase in mental conditions as per our conducted health survey.

Volunteer to man the helpline:

  • At MentHealth we offer support from professionally trained and skilled volunteers.
  • The volunteers are selected with great care, chosen for their ability to listen with empathy and understanding.
  • Each volunteer has to undergo training for a minimum of six months before they can start dealing with callers.

Volunteer for publicity and fundraising:

  • Help us spread awareness!
  • Students can talk about and represent MentHealth in their schools and colleges.
  • Bloggers can blog about us in their community, link us or help advertise MentHealth by giving us a small space in their blog or website.
  • Alternatively, a volunteer can use their Twitter/Facebook or any other social networking platform to promote MentHealth among friends and family.
  • A volunteer can direct potential donors to MentHealth.
  • As for Corporates, we would be thrilled if you can adopt MentHealth as a part of your "Corporate Social Responsibility".

MentHealth functions as a unit of Befrienders Worldwide/Samaritans and is a non-religious, non-political and non-sectarian organization, registered as a Public Charity under the Indian Charity Act, 1960 (Registration No. E 2047). Befrienders Worldwide/Samaritans has been working in the field of crisis intervention/areas of emotional and mental health since 1960. MentHealth is the only help line in Bengaluru and started functioning from 21st July,1999.
